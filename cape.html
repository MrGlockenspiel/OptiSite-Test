Change Cape

To activate, deactivate, transfer, or simply change the design of your OptiFine cape:

  1. Download and install the latest version of OptiFine. Any version released on or after December 4, 2019 will work.
  2. Open Minecraft with OptiFine.
  3. Open the Options menu and navigate to
    Skin Customization -> OptiFine Cape -> Open Cape Editor

From there, your browser should automatically open with the cape editor page.
